# Moon Design System

A versatile design system for building consistent, fast and engaging experiences.<br/>
Head over to the [playground](playground) to get started.

## Why?

### Consistency

Consistent experiences are key to customer satisfaction; familiarity breeds brand trust and increases engagement.

Re-using our code allows us to ensure the highest standards of testing, accessibility and performance.

### Scalability

Our design system is built for scale and experimentation; allowing us to test and roll out new ideas seamlessly.
