### Group Icons

When consuming several icons at once, you may want to consider using an icon
"group" shortcut.

#### Third Party

```jsx react-live
<Inline fontSize="2rem">
  <GroupIconThirdParty name="bitcointalk" />
  <GroupIconThirdParty name="facebook" />
  <GroupIconThirdParty name="google" />
  <GroupIconThirdParty name="instagram" />
  <GroupIconThirdParty name="line" />
  <GroupIconThirdParty name="telegram" />
  <GroupIconThirdParty name="twitter" />
  <GroupIconThirdParty name="vkontakte" />
  <GroupIconThirdParty name="youtube" />
</Inline>
```
